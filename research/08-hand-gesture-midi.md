## What It Is

Plays MIDI chords based on hand gestures using OpenCV and cvzone. Similar in concept to Air-Piano — finger position detection mapped to chord playback.

**Stack:** OpenCV + cvzone + (likely pygame.midi, given cvzone usage pattern)

## Relevance

Low differentiation from Air-Piano (#3 in this list). Both use cvzone for hand detection and map finger state to chord playback. Air-Piano is better documented, more starred, and more feature-complete.
