## What It Is

An early (2013) ML-based tool to generate MIDI tones from hand movements, written in Processing.

## Relevance

Historical interest only — this predates MediaPipe by ~6 years and uses older CV techniques. The ML-from-hand-movements concept was ahead of its time. Owner has 51 followers but the project is over a decade old and the Processing ecosystem has largely been superseded.

Not worth further investigation for a current build, but notable as an early example of the ML classification approach that solfege.ai (#19) later formalized with TensorFlow.
