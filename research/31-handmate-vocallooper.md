## What It Is

A gesture-controlled vocal looper and MIDI beat maker for vocalists, podcasters, and performers. Built on the Handmate approach (MediaPipe + WebMidi), extended with audio looping capabilities.

**Features (per README):**
- Multi-track vocal looping (record and layer multiple tracks)
- Real-time audio effects: reverb, delay, pitch correction
- MIDI beat making
- Live performance UI

**Stack:** HTML/JS + npm (Node.js local server) — same pattern as Handmate-MIDI-Offline
