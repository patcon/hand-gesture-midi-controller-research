## What It Is

A browser-based software MIDI pad controller and sample player. Described as a DAW that "turns any modern web browser into a professional MIDI pad controller." No gesture/hand tracking — this is a touchscreen/mouse-driven software instrument.

## Relevance

Not a gesture recognition project. Not relevant to the core use case. Low owner social trust.
