## What It Is

A setup guide and configuration for connecting a Leap Motion controller to Ableton Live using **Tekh Map** and **MIDI Paw** — two proprietary middleware tools. Not a code project; it's a documented workflow using existing commercial software.

## Relevance

Low direct relevance — no source code, requires Leap Motion hardware and paid/closed tools. Low owner social trust. Useful only as a reference for what the end-user experience of Leap Motion → Ableton looks like, and as background context on the middleware layer that exists between gesture input and DAWs.

## Notes

- Tekh Map and MIDI Paw are the interesting discovery here — worth knowing these exist as commercial alternatives to what we're building
