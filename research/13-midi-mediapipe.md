## What It Is

Uses MediaPipe **pose detection** (full body, not just hands) to send data to a MIDI port. Framed as an "Ableton Live experiment."

**Stack:** MediaPipe (Pose) + MIDI output (library unspecified)

## Relevance

Interesting for the combination of pose detection + direct Ableton targeting. Only the second project in this list (alongside SynesthesIA) to use full-body pose rather than hand tracking only. Low stars and low owner social trust, but recently active (Feb 2026).

The "Ableton experiment" framing suggests practical DAW integration testing rather than a polished tool. Worth a quick look at the code to see how pose landmarks are mapped to MIDI.

## Notes

- Full-body pose → MIDI is an underexplored area relative to hand-only approaches
- Complementary to SynesthesIA (#7) which also targets pose
